Lanzarote is a popular holiday destination. The restaurants offer wonderful food, the attractions offer unforgettable memories, however, there are lesser known experiences to be enjoyed. El Golfo – the green lagoon, after a small trek the lagoon offers stunning views of the coast and surrounding areas. The relaxing beach bar Luca allows you to enjoy a peaceful drink with tranquil music. Pueblo Marinero is right at the heart of Costa Teguise and allows you to really sense the atmosphere of the island – great for a few drinks after a lovely day. La Graciosa is a small boat journey away and is the least explored in all the Canary Islands. Bodegas Rubicon is a winery over 300 years old that perfectly blends tradition with innovation.
