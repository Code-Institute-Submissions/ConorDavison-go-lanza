There are always places to visit in Lanzarote. This volcanic island was plenty of history and culture. Lanzarote has a wide variety of attractions to visit, see the images above for just a selection of possible excursions. The cactus garden makes for a tranquil visit while the cave under the volcano can be visited for those who cant to explore further! Jameos del Agua is a place where mankind and nature come together, the pristine pool water and deep caves help visitors enjoy a personal experience difficult to forget. A must visit when in Lanzarote is Timanfaya, located in the Fire Mountains. This is the most popular attraction on the island and is were the beauty of Lanzarote can be truly realised.
